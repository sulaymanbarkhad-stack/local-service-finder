How SomaliConnect Works

Connecting customers with trusted local service providers has never been easier. Whether you're
looking for services or offering them, SomaliConnect makes it simple.

01

Search for Services

Browse our extensive directory of service providers by category or use
the search function to find exactly what you need. Filter by location,
ratings, and availability.

02

Compare Providers

View detailed profiles including ratings, reviews, experience, and pricing.
Read feedback from other customers in your community to make an
informed decision.

03

Contact directly

Once you've found the right provider, contact them directly via email.
Discuss your needs, get quotes, and schedule services at your
convenience.

04

Leave a Review

After the service is complete, share your experience by leaving a review.
Help other customers make better decisions and support quality service
providers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SomaliConnect free to use?

Yes! Customers can browse and contact service providers completely free. Service providers can also create
profiles at no cost.

How are service providers verified?

We verify service providers through document checks, reference verification, and community feedback to
ensure quality and trustworthiness.

What areas does SomaliConnect cover?

We currently serve major cities across Somalia including Mogadishu, Hargeisa, Kismayo, Baidoa, and Garowe,
with plans to expand to more areas.

How do I leave a review?

After using a service, you can return to the provider's profile and leave a rating and review to help other
customers make informed decisions.
